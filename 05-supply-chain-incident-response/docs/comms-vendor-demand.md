# Vendor Demand for Information — Incident Response

**To:** [Vendor Security Contact]
**From:** [SMG Risk Owner]
**Date:** [Date]
**Re:** Security Incident — Demand for Information
**Priority:** URGENT — Response required within [24/48] hours

---

[Vendor Name],

We have been made aware of a security incident affecting your organization. Under the terms of our agreement [reference contract section], we require the following information:

1. **Timeline:** When did the incident begin? When was it discovered? When was it contained?
2. **Scope:** What systems, services, and data were affected?
3. **Our Exposure:** Was data belonging to [Organization Name] accessed, exfiltrated, or otherwise compromised? If yes, what specific data and how many records?
4. **Containment:** What immediate containment actions have been taken?
5. **Root Cause:** What is the preliminary root cause? If investigation is ongoing, when will root cause analysis be available?
6. **Remediation:** What remediation steps have been taken or are planned?
7. **Forensic Investigation:** Has a third-party forensic firm been engaged? If yes, when will the forensic report be available?
8. **Ongoing Risk:** Are there any ongoing risks to our data or systems?

We require a substantive response within **[24/48] hours** of this notice. Failure to respond within this timeframe will be documented and factored into our ongoing vendor risk assessment.

We reserve the right to activate the audit clause in our agreement to independently verify the scope and impact of this incident.
