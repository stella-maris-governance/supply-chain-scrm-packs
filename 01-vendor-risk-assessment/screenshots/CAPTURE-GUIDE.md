# Screenshot Capture Guide — Vendor Risk Assessment

---

### 01 — Vendor Risk Register
**Path:** SharePoint > VendorRiskRegister list (or GRC tool)
**Show:** All vendors with tier, score, approval status, next re-assessment

### 02 — Intake Form Completed
**Path:** SharePoint > New vendor intake form
**Show:** Completed intake for a Critical-tier vendor with all fields

### 03 — Risk Scoring Breakdown
**Path:** `calculate_risk.py` output or GRC tool
**Show:** 6-domain breakdown for a High vendor with weighted scores

### 04 — Approval Workflow
**Path:** Power Automate > Vendor-Approval-Workflow run history
**Show:** Conditional acceptance with compensating controls documented

### 05 — Re-Assessment Dashboard
**Path:** SharePoint view or KQL output
**Show:** Vendors by tier with re-assessment due dates and status
